Garlic Broccoli Rabe

This dish is simple and a great addition to any entree you make. The veg pops with garlicky flavor that leaves your mouth wanting more.

    Ingredients
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 1 bunch broccoli rabe (~1 pound)
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove, thinly sliced
  • 1 small dried red chile pepper

    Directions
  1. Bring 4 quarts water to a rolling boil in a stockpot
  2. Cut broccoli into 1 inch pieces
  3. ADd to the boiling water and boil for 2 minutes, then drain and cool slightly
  4. Squeeze the moisture out of the leaves
  5. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  6. Add the garlic and red chile pepper
  7. Add the broccoli rabe and cook, stirring occasionally until tender, ~4 min
  8. Remove from heat and discard chile
  9. Season with salt and pepper
