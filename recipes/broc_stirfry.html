Broccoli Stir Fry

This broccoli stir fry is a fun way to get broccoli onto the table. The mix of flavors combine to make a DELICIOUS sauce that covers the crispy broccoli. Plus you get to cook this tasty meal in a Wok. What's not to love!

    Ingredients
  • 2 pounds steamed broccoli
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/3 cup Shaoxing wine or dry sherry
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 slices fresh peeled ginger
  • 4 large garlic cloves crushed

    Directions
  1. Steam the broccoli until crisp and tender, then dry and set aside
  2. Stir together the water and the cornstarch until smooth
  3. Combine the wine, soy sauce, light brown sugar, and toasted sesame oil in a small bowl
  4. Place a Wok over high heat
  5. When it gets hot ass the vegetable oil and swirl until it gets very hot
  6. Add the ginger and garlic cloves
  7. Stir and press with the back of a spoon until the garlic is lightly golden, then remove and discard the ginger and garlic
  8. Add the broccoli and stir-fry until lightly covered in oil
  9. Pour wine mixture down the side of the pan and toss
  10. Reduce the heat, cover the wok, and cook for 30 seconds
  11. Stir the cornstarch mixture, add it to the wok, and stir to combine
  12. Cook uncovered until the sauce thickens, 1-2 minutes
